# Reminders

One-shot and recurring reminders that notify you (and whoever you choose) at the
right time.

## What you can do
- **Set a reminder** for a specific time/date, or a recurring one.
- **Choose recipients** so a reminder pings the right family member(s).
- **Get notified** through your configured channels (web, Discord, mobile push).
- **Ask Skipper** — "remind me to take the trash out at 7pm", "remind Jess about
  the dentist next Tuesday at 2", or "what reminders do I have today?".

## Tips
- The lead time (how far ahead it fires) is configurable in Settings → Reminders.
- For repeating household tasks, the **Chores** or **Schedules** apps may fit better.
