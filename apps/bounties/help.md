# Bounties

Reward-based tasks for the family — post a bounty with a cash value, let kids
claim and complete it, and track payouts.

## What you can do
- **Post bounties** (a task + a dollar value) from bounty templates or one-offs.
- **Claim & complete** — a family member does the work and marks it done.
- **Approve & pay** — a parent confirms completion and records the payment.
- **Leaderboard** — see who's earned what over time.
- **Ask Skipper** — "post a $5 bounty to clean the garage", or "who's on top of
  the bounty leaderboard this month?".

## Tips
- Parents/admins control creating bounties and recording payments.
- Set a minimum payout in the app's settings (Settings → Bounties).
