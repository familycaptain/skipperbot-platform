# Thinking

A window into Skipper's autonomous "thinking" — the background reasoning loops
(thinking domains) that work on your goals, reminders, and more between chats.

## Overview

Beyond answering you in chat, Skipper runs background **thinking domains** — e.g.
the Project Manager that nudges goals forward. Thinking is a read-only viewer into
that: what it's been working on, when domains ran, their working memory and
reasoning log, and the token budget they use. It's mainly for understanding *why*
Skipper did something proactively.

## Screens

- **Domains.** The active thinking domains and when each last ran.
- **Activity / reasoning log.** A domain's recent runs and what it concluded.
- **Working memory & budget.** What a domain is currently holding in mind and its
  token budget.

## Example workflows

**See what Skipper's been up to**
- *In the app:* open Thinking to watch the domains and their recent activity.
- *Through chat:* "what have you been thinking about?", "why did the PM nudge me
  about the garage?"

**Understand a proactive action**
- *In the app:* open the relevant domain's log to see the reasoning behind a nudge.

## Tips

- This is a **viewer/debugger** — the domains themselves live with their owning
  apps (e.g. the PM domain is part of **Goals**).
- Use it when a proactive message surprises you and you want the "why".

## Your data

Thinking **owns no records you create** — it displays Skipper's own reasoning
state and logs. There's nothing here for you to add; it's a read-only look into
how Skipper works.
