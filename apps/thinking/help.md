# Thinking

A window into Skipper's autonomous "thinking" — the background reasoning loops
(thinking domains) that proactively work on goals, reminders, and more.

## What you can do
- **Watch** what Skipper is thinking about and when domains run.
- **Inspect** a domain's recent activity to understand why it did something.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what have you been thinking about?", or "why did the PM nudge
  me?".

## Tips
- This is a viewer/debugger; the thinking domains themselves live with their
  owning apps (e.g. the PM domain in Goals).
