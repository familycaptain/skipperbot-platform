# Automation

Connect smart-home devices and automations (e.g. via Home Assistant) so Skipper
can see and control them.

## What you can do
- **Control devices** — lights, switches, and other connected gear.
- **Reference scenes/automations** from your smart-home setup.
- **Ask Skipper** — "turn off the living room lights", or "is the garage door
  closed?".

## Setup
- Requires connecting your smart-home hub in Settings → Automation; see
  `docs/03-extended-functionality.md`. Without it, this app stays idle.
