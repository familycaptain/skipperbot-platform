# Folders

Organize related items into folders — group documents, notes, and other entities
however makes sense to you.

## What you can do
- **Create folders** and put items into them.
- **Browse** a folder to see everything grouped under it.
- **Ask Skipper** — "make a folder for the kitchen remodel and put these docs in
  it", or "what's in the taxes folder?".

## Tips
- Folders cut across apps — a folder can hold different kinds of items together.
