# Behaviors

Tune how Skipper acts — configurable rules that shape its tone, proactiveness,
and conventions.

## What you can do
- **Add/edit behavior rules** that Skipper follows in conversations and actions.
- **Ask Skipper** — "always confirm before deleting things", or "be more concise
  in chat".

## Tips
- Behaviors are house rules for the assistant; keep them short and specific.
