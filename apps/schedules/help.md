# Schedules

Recurring schedules and a calendar view of everything happening — appointments,
recurring events, and due items across your apps.

## What you can do
- **Create recurring schedules** (daily, weekly, monthly, custom rules).
- **See a calendar** that aggregates schedule occurrences, reminders, tasks with
  due dates, and more in one place.
- **Ask Skipper** — "schedule soccer practice every Tuesday and Thursday at 5",
  "what's on the calendar this weekend?", or "add a monthly bill-pay reminder".

## Tips
- Schedules are the platform's single source of recurring work — there's no cron
  to manage; everything recurring flows through here.
- One-time nudges are usually better as **Reminders**.
