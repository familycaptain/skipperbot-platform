# Scriptures

A Bible reader for study — browse, search, bookmark, highlight, and take notes on
scripture, across multiple translations.

## Overview

Scriptures lets you read the Bible inside Skipper and build up a personal study
layer on top: bookmarks for places you want to return to, highlights on verses
that stand out, and study notes. It can load multiple Bible versions (from MySword
modules), so you can compare translations. You can navigate in the app or just ask
Skipper for a passage.

## Screens

- **Reader.** Navigate by book → chapter → verse; switch translation/version.
- **Search.** Find verses by word or phrase.
- **Bookmarks & highlights.** Your saved places and highlighted verses.
- **Notes.** Study notes you've attached to passages.

## Example workflows

**Read a passage**
- *In the app:* navigate to the book/chapter, or use search.
- *Through chat:* "show me Psalm 23", "open John 3".

**Search scripture**
- *In the app:* search a word/phrase.
- *Through chat:* "find the verse about being still", "what does Proverbs say
  about diligence?"

**Study layer**
- *In the app:* bookmark a chapter, highlight a verse, add a note.
- Compare translations by switching the version.

## Tips

- Load additional versions (MySword modules) to compare translations side by side.
- Pairs well with the **Documents** app for longer study write-ups.

## Your data

Your **bookmarks, highlights, and study notes are saved in the database and
pulled into Skipper's memory**, so you can ask "what did I highlight in Romans?"
later. They stay within your household; the Bible text itself comes from the
loaded version modules.
