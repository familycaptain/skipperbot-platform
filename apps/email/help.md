# Email

Connect an email account so Skipper can triage, summarize, and act on mail with
rules you define.

## What you can do
- **Connect an account** (e.g. Gmail) in Settings → Email.
- **Define rules** for how mail is handled.
- **Ask Skipper** — "any important emails today?", "summarize my unread mail", or
  "flag anything from the school".

## Setup
- Requires connecting your mail provider and granting access; see Settings →
  Email and `docs/03-extended-functionality.md`. Without it, this app stays idle.
