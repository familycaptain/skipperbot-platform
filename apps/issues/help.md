# Issues

Report bugs and request features for your Skipper install. Issues are the work
queue Skipper (via Evolve / Claude Code) and your operator work from.

## What you can do
- **Log a bug or feature** with a title and description; attach screenshots.
- **Track status** as it moves: open → in progress → pending validation → fixed
  (or won't fix / duplicate).
- **Validate fixes** — when an issue is marked pending validation, confirm it
  works and it gets closed.
- **Ask Skipper** — "report a bug: the calendar is showing the wrong day", or
  "request a feature to export recipes to PDF".

## Tips
- Clear, specific reports get resolved faster — say what you expected vs. saw.
- This app powers the self-improvement loop described in the Evolve spec.
