# Recipes

Your family's recipe box — store, browse, search, and cook from a collection of
recipes with structured ingredients, steps, categories, and photos.

## What you can do
- **Add recipes** with a title, ingredients, step-by-step instructions, prep/cook
  times, servings, and a photo.
- **Organize** recipes into categories (e.g. dinners, desserts, breakfasts).
- **Search** by name, ingredient, or category to find something to make.
- **Ask Skipper** in chat — "what can I make with chicken and rice?", "add mom's
  lasagna recipe", or "show me quick weeknight dinners" — and it uses this app.

## Tips
- Scale a recipe by asking Skipper to adjust servings.
- Recipes pair with the **Meals** app for planning what to cook and when.
- Add a photo to make the recipe list easy to scan.
