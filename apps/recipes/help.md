# Recipes

Your family's recipe box — store, browse, search, and cook from recipes with
structured ingredients, steps, categories, and photos.

## Overview

Recipes keeps your collection as proper structured recipes (not free-form notes):
each has ingredients, step-by-step instructions, a category, optional rating, and
a photo. Add them by hand or just paste/tell Skipper a recipe and it files it.
Pairs with **Meals** (deciding what to cook) and **Lists** (the shopping run).

## Screens

- **Recipe browser.** Your recipes as a scannable list/grid (photos help); filter
  by category, search by name or ingredient.
- **Recipe detail.** Ingredients, steps, times/servings, category, rating, image.
- **Categories.** Organize recipes (Dinners, Desserts, Sauces, Mexican, …).

## Example workflows

**Add a recipe**
- *In the app:* create a recipe — title, ingredients, steps, category, photo.
- *Through chat:* paste the recipe text or say "add mom's lasagna recipe" and
  Skipper parses it into a structured recipe.

**Find something to make**
- *In the app:* search/filter the browser.
- *Through chat:* "what can I make with chicken and rice?", "show me quick
  weeknight dinners", "open the lasagna recipe".

**Adjust / organize**
- *Through chat:* "scale the lasagna to 8 servings", "put this under Desserts".

## Tips

- Add a photo so the browser is easy to scan.
- Use **Meals** to plan which recipes to cook and **Lists** to shop for them.
- Recipes are structured entities — searchable by ingredient, not just title.

## Your data

Your recipes, categories, ratings, and images are **saved in the database and
pulled into Skipper's memory**, so "what can I make with…?" and "show me that
lasagna recipe" work any time. They stay within your household.
