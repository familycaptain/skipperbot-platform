# Locator

Remember where things are — track where you stored items so you can find them
later.

## What you can do
- **Record item locations** ("passports are in the fireproof box in the closet").
- **Look things up** when you can't remember where you put them.
- **Ask Skipper** — "where are the Christmas decorations?", or "remember that the
  spare key is under the third flowerpot".

## Tips
- Great for seasonal items, important documents, and rarely-used gear.
