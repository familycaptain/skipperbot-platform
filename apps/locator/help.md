# Locator

Never lose track of where you put things — record where physical items are stored
and find them later by location, category, tag, or search.

## Overview

Locator is a "where did I put that?" tracker for the household. Record an item and
where it lives, optionally with a category, tags, and a photo. Later, look it up
by any of those — perfect for seasonal decorations, important documents, spare
parts, and rarely-used gear.

## Screens

- **Items.** Your tracked items with their locations; filter by location,
  category, or tag, and search by name.
- **Item detail.** An item's location, category, tags, notes, and photo.
- **Locations.** The places you store things (closet, garage, fireproof box, …).

## Example workflows

**Record where something is**
- *In the app:* add an item, set its location (and category/tags/photo).
- *Through chat:* "the passports are in the fireproof box in the closet",
  "remember the spare key is under the third flowerpot".

**Find it later**
- *In the app:* search or filter by location/category/tag.
- *Through chat:* "where are the Christmas decorations?", "where did I put the
  good scissors?"

**Move something**
- *Through chat:* "the camping gear moved to the attic" → updates its location.

## Tips

- Great for things you touch rarely — seasonal items, documents, tools, spare parts.
- Attach a photo so the item (and its spot) is unmistakable.

## Your data

Items, their locations, categories, tags, and photos are **saved in the database
and pulled into Skipper's memory**, so asking "where's the X?" works even months
later. It stays within your household.
