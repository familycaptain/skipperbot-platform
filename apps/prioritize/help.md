# Prioritize

Cut through the noise — pull your most important items into focus and manage a
backlog of what to do next.

## What you can do
- **Set focus** — surface the handful of things that matter most right now.
- **Manage a backlog** of tasks/items pulled from across your apps.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what should I focus on today?", or "add this to my backlog".

## Tips
- Works across Goals, To-Do, and other apps to assemble one prioritized view.
