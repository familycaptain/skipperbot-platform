# Finance Calculator

Two calculators in one — **compound interest** (savings/investment growth) and
**loan amortization** (payment / payoff). Enter the values you know, leave
exactly one field blank, and it solves for the missing one.

## Compound interest
Fields: Principal, Annual rate, Compounds/year (e.g. 12 = monthly), Years,
Future value. Leave one of Principal / Rate / Years / Future value blank.
- "How much will $10,000 grow to in 10 years at 6%?" → leave Future value blank.
- "What rate do I need to double my money in 8 years?" → leave Rate blank.

## Loan / amortization
Fields: Loan amount, Annual rate, Term (years), Monthly payment. Leave one blank.
- "What's the payment on a $250k, 30-year loan at 6.5%?" → leave Payment blank.
- "How long to pay off if I pay $2,000/month?" → leave Term blank.
- Shows monthly payment, total interest, total paid, and a full month-by-month
  amortization schedule (balance, principal, interest per payment).

## Tips
- Everything runs in your browser — no data is stored.
- Rate is the one value with no exact formula for loans, so it's solved
  numerically (still accurate to the penny).
