# Finder

Universal search across everything in Skipper — find items no matter which app
they live in.

## What you can do
- **Search once**, get results from every app (recipes, documents, goals, lists,
  reminders, and more).
- **Jump** straight to a result's app.
- **Ask Skipper** — or just type in Finder — "find anything about the beach trip".

## Tips
- Use Finder when you're not sure which app something is in.
