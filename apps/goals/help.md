# Goals

Plan and track goals, projects, and tasks. Skipper's Project Manager (PM) can
own goals and proactively help move them forward.

## How it's organized
- **Goals** — the big outcomes you want.
- **Projects** — chunks of work under a goal.
- **Tasks** (and subtasks) — the concrete to-dos under a project.

## What you can do
- Create goals/projects/tasks, set owners, due dates, and priorities.
- Assign work to family members — or to **Skipper**, which activates the PM
  thinking domain so Skipper actively tracks and nudges progress.
- **Ask Skipper** — "start a goal to plan our summer trip", "what tasks are due
  this week?", or "have Skipper own getting the garage organized".

## Tips
- Anything owned/assigned to Skipper gets proactive PM follow-up at its normal
  cadence (it nudges rather than nags, and respects quiet mode in settings).
- The onboarding goal you see on a new install is created this way.
