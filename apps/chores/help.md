# Chores

Track household chores and who's responsible for them, with recurring schedules
and completion tracking.

## What you can do
- **Define chores** and assign them to family members.
- **Set recurrence** (daily, weekly, specific days) so chores reappear on cadence.
- **Mark chores done** and see what's outstanding.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what chores does Caleb have today?", "mark trash taken out",
  or "add a weekly chore to vacuum the living room".

## Tips
- Chores work with reminders/notifications so the right person gets nudged.
- Use clear, action-style names ("Empty dishwasher") so completions read well.
