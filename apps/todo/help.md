# To-Do

Your personal "when I get to it" list — quick tasks with no due dates or ceremony.

## Overview

To-Do is a lightweight personal list, one per person. Jot things down, check them
off — simpler than a full Goals project and not tied to a clock like Reminders.
Under the hood it's a single list from the **Lists** app presented as your
personal to-do (with an optional backlog list), plus a gentle weekly nudge to
triage it.

## Screens

- **My to-do.** Your default list — add items, check them off, reorder.
- **Backlog (optional).** A second list for "someday/maybe" items you're not
  acting on yet.

## Example workflows

**Capture a task**
- *In the app:* type it into your to-do and hit add.
- *Through chat:* "add 'fix the squeaky door' to my to-do".

**Work the list**
- *In the app:* check items off as you do them.
- *Through chat:* "what's on my to-do?", "I finished the squeaky door" (marks it done).

**Weekly triage**
- A weekly nudge prompts a quick review — clear what's done, move stale items to
  the backlog.

## Tips

- For time-sensitive things use **Reminders**; for multi-step efforts use **Goals**.
- Pull your most important to-dos into **Prioritize** to focus on them.
- Named lists ("the shopping list") belong to **Lists** — To-Do is just *your* one list.

## Your data

Your to-do items live in the **Lists** store (this app is a personal lens over
one list), so they're **saved in the database and pulled into Skipper's memory** —
"what's on my to-do?" works any time. They stay within your household.
