# To-Do

Lightweight "when I have time" tasks — a personal backlog that's simpler than a
full Goals project. Built on the Lists engine.

## What you can do
- **Jot down tasks** quickly without due dates or ceremony.
- **Check them off** as you get to them.
- **Ask Skipper** — "add 'fix the squeaky door' to my to-do", or "what's on my
  to-do list?".

## Tips
- For time-sensitive items use **Reminders**; for multi-step efforts use **Goals**.
- Use **Prioritize** to pull the most important items into focus.
