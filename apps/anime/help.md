# Anime

Track and watch anime — browse a catalog, keep a watchlist, and follow your
progress through series.

## What you can do
- **Search** the catalog and add shows to your list.
- **Track progress** through episodes and seasons.
- **Ask Skipper** — "add this show to my watchlist", or "what was I watching?".

## Tips
- Built on a public anime catalog; availability depends on the upstream source.
