# Auto Maintenance

Keep track of your vehicles and their upkeep — service records, open issues, and
maintenance history.

## What you can do
- **Add vehicles** and record details about each.
- **Log service records** (oil changes, tires, repairs) with dates and notes.
- **Track issues** a vehicle is having and their resolution.
- **Ask Skipper** — "log an oil change for the Honda at 92,000 miles", "when did
  we last rotate the tires?", or "what's overdue on the truck?".

## Tips
- Recurring maintenance pairs well with the **Schedules** app for reminders.
