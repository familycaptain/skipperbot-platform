# Backups

Protect your data — scheduled database backups, with optional off-site copies.

## What you can do
- **See backup history** and whether the latest one succeeded.
- **Configure destinations** in Settings → Backups: local filesystem and/or
  Google Drive (each independently toggleable).
- **Ask Skipper** — "did last night's backup run?", or "run a backup now".

## Setup
- Works out of the box for local backups; Google Drive needs credentials in
  Settings → Backups. The platform runs/checks backups on a schedule.
