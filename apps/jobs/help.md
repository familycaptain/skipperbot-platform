# Jobs

A viewer for Skipper's background work — research runs, backups, analyses, and
other queued jobs.

## What you can do
- **See running, queued, and finished jobs** with their status and results.
- **Check why** a job failed.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what background jobs are running?", or "did the research job
  finish?".

## Tips
- Most jobs are kicked off automatically by other apps and thinking domains;
  this is the window into them.
