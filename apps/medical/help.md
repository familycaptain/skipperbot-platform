# Medical

A private record of your family's health — members, events, medications,
treatments, and lab results.

## What you can do
- **Track each family member's** medical history.
- **Log events** (visits, symptoms, diagnoses), **medications**, **treatments**
  and treatment logs, and **lab tests/results**.
- **Ask Skipper** — "log that Caleb started amoxicillin today", "when was Jess's
  last physical?", or "show Caleb's medication history".

## Tips
- This is sensitive data — it stays in your self-hosted database.
- For homeopathic remedies specifically, see the **Homeopathy** app.
