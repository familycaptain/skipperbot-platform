# Timeline

A single activity feed of everything that's changed across Skipper — who did
what, when, in which app.

## What you can do
- **Scroll the feed** to see recent changes from every app in one place.
- **Use it as an audit trail** — "what changed in my Skipper recently?".
- **Ask Skipper** — "what happened today?", or "show recent activity".

## Tips
- Most apps emit timeline entries automatically; this is your at-a-glance history.
