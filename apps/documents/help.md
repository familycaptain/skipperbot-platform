# Documents

Long-form markdown documents — notes, plans, research, specs, anything that
doesn't fit a structured app. Skipper can read, write, and edit them.

## What you can do
- **Create and edit** markdown documents in a rich editor.
- **Have Skipper draft** documents for you and revise them inline.
- **Organize** documents and link them to other items.
- **Ask Skipper** — "write up a packing checklist as a document", "summarize
  this into a new doc", or "open my vacation plan".

## Tips
- Documents are where Skipper co-authors specs and plans with you (see Evolve).
