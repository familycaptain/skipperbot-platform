# Meals

Plan what the family eats — build meals from recipes and components, organize by
cuisine and tags, and decide what's for dinner.

## What you can do
- **Compose meals** from recipes (from the Recipes app) and standalone components.
- **Tag and categorize** by cuisine and meal tags for easy filtering.
- **Plan ahead** — decide upcoming meals and what to shop for.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what's for dinner tonight?", "plan three Italian dinners
  this week", or "build a taco night meal".

## Tips
- Pairs with **Recipes** (the actual recipes) and **Lists** (shopping lists).
