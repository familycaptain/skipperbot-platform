# Homeopathy

Track homeopathic remedies and your supply — medicines, remedies, bottles and
their sizes, storage locations, and sources.

## What you can do
- **Catalog remedies and medicines** you keep on hand.
- **Track bottles** (size, location, source) so you know what you have and where.
- **Ask Skipper** — "do we have arnica 30c?", "where is the bottle of nux vomica?",
  or "add a new remedy I just bought".

## Tips
- For conventional medications and medical history, use the **Medical** app.
