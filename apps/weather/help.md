# Weather

A local weather dashboard for your area.

## What it shows
- **Current conditions** — temperature, feels-like, humidity, wind, and UV index.
- **Next 12 hours** — an hourly strip with temperature, conditions, and rain chance.
- **10-day forecast** — daily highs/lows, conditions, and rain chance.

## Using it
- It opens to your **home ZIP code** (set in Settings → System → Default ZIP code).
- Type any US ZIP in the box and press **Go** to look somewhere else.
- Use the refresh button to re-pull the latest data.

- **Radar & alerts map** — a ~100-mile view around your ZIP with live NEXRAD
  radar and active NWS severe-weather alert areas (click an alert for details).

Weather data comes from open-meteo and needs no API key. Nothing is stored.

You can also just ask Skipper in chat — "what's the weather", "what's the
forecast", "what's the daily forecast", "chance of rain today" — which use the
same data.
