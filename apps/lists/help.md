# Lists

General-purpose lists — shopping lists, packing lists, anything you want to check
off. Optionally synced with Trello.

## What you can do
- **Create lists** and add/check off items.
- **Share** lists across the family.
- **Trello sync** (optional) — connect Trello boards in Settings → Lists to mirror
  lists/cards both ways.
- **Ask Skipper** — "add milk and eggs to the grocery list", "make a packing list
  for the beach trip", or "what's left on the hardware-store list?".

## Tips
- The **To-Do** app is built on Lists for personal "when I have time" tasks.
