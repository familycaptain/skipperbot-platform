# Lists

General-purpose lists — shopping, packing, anything you check off — in the order
you want, optionally synced to Trello.

## Overview

A list is an ordered collection of items you can add to, reorder, and check off.
Lists can be shared across the family, and any list can be **linked to a Trello
board/list** so adds/removes sync both ways (Trello stays the source of truth for
linked lists). The **To-Do** app is built on top of Lists (it's a personal "Todo"
list per person).

## Screens

- **Lists.** Your lists; open one to see its items.
- **List view.** Items in drag-reorderable order — add, check off, remove, reorder.
- **Trello (optional).** Connect a Trello board to mirror its lists/cards here.

## Example workflows

**Make a list and fill it**
- *In the app:* create a list, add items, drag to reorder, check off as you go.
- *Through chat:* "make a packing list for the beach trip", "add milk and eggs to
  the grocery list", "what's left on the hardware-store list?"

**Check things off**
- *In the app:* tap an item to check it.
- *Through chat:* "check off milk on the grocery list".

**Sync with Trello (optional)**
- *Through chat:* "connect my shopping Trello board" → it creates a local list per
  Trello list and syncs cards; after that, "add screws to Hobby Lobby" writes
  through to Trello automatically.

## Tips

- Linked lists treat Trello as the source of truth — edits here write through to it.
- For personal "when I get to it" tasks, use **To-Do** (it's a Lists-backed view).

## Your data

Your lists and their items are **saved in the database and pulled into Skipper's
memory**, so you can ask "what's on the grocery list?" any time. Trello-linked
lists also live on your Trello board; everything else stays within your household.
