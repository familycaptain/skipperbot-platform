# Brainstorming

A space for ideas that aren't yet goals — capture an idea, break it into parts,
sketch flowcharts, and develop it with Skipper before it becomes real work.

## What you can do
- **Create ideas** with multiple parts (documents, flowcharts, links, images).
- **Draw flowcharts** to map out how something could work.
- **Develop ideas** in conversation with Skipper; graduate a mature idea into a
  Goal when you're ready to act.
- **Ask Skipper** — "brainstorm names for the new puppy", or "help me flesh out
  a budgeting idea".

## Tips
- Flowchart edits autosave; if a node ever seems missing, reopen the document.
