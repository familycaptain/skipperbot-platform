# Brainstorming

A place for ideas that aren't yet real work — capture an idea, develop it with
notes and flowcharts, and graduate it into a Goal when it's ready.

## Overview

Brainstorming is the "thinking out loud" space. Each idea is more than a title:
it carries a structured **document** you build up over time, optional
**flowcharts** to map how something could work, plus a status, priority, and
tags. You develop ideas in conversation with Skipper, and when one is solid, you
graduate it into the **Goals** app to actually act on it.

## Screens

- **Idea list.** Browse/search your ideas, filtered by status, priority, or tags.
- **Idea detail.** An idea's working document (markdown you and Skipper edit) and
  its flowchart canvas; set status/priority/tags here.
- **Flowchart.** Drag-and-drop nodes to sketch a flow; edits autosave.

## Example workflows

**Capture and develop an idea**
- *In the app:* create an idea, then add notes/sections to its document and sketch
  a flowchart.
- *Through chat:* "start an idea about a backyard garden", then "add a section on
  raised-bed layouts to the garden idea".

**Brainstorm with Skipper**
- *Through chat:* "brainstorm names for the new puppy", "help me flesh out a
  budgeting idea" — Skipper expands the idea's document with you.

**Turn an idea into action**
- *Through chat:* "graduate the garden idea into a goal" → it becomes a Goal in
  the Goals app with its content carried over.

## Tips

- Use **Brainstorming** for half-formed ideas; use **Goals** once you're committed
  to doing it.
- Flowchart edits autosave — if a node ever looks missing, reopen the idea.

## Your data

Your ideas, their documents, flowcharts, statuses, and tags are **saved in the
database and pulled into Skipper's memory** — so you can ask "what was that
budgeting idea?" later and Skipper recalls it. It stays within your household.
