# Home

Home maintenance and household upkeep — track recurring home tasks and the issues
your house develops over time.

## What you can do
- **Track home tasks** (filter the furnace, clean gutters, test smoke detectors)
  with recurrence.
- **Log home issues** (a leak, a broken fixture) and follow them to resolution.
- **Ask Skipper** — "add a quarterly task to replace the HVAC filter", "log that
  the upstairs faucet is dripping", or "what home maintenance is due this month?".

## Tips
- Recurring tasks lean on the **Schedules**/**Reminders** apps for nudges.
