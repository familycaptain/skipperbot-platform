# Notifications

One place for everything Skipper wants to tell you — reminders that fired, jobs
that finished, PM nudges — delivered across whatever channels you've turned on.

## Overview

Notifications is the household's alert center. Other apps and thinking domains
record "tell the user X" events here, and a delivery loop pushes each one out via
your configured channels — in-app, Discord DM, Pushover, and mobile push. You
don't create notifications yourself; they're generated by the rest of the system.
Each person chooses which channels they want.

## Screens

- **Notifications list.** Recent alerts from every app, newest first, with where
  each came from (a reminder, a job, a PM nudge) and whether it was delivered.
- Filter by source or time; mark as read.

## Setup

In-app notifications work with no setup. To also get them on **Discord**,
**Pushover**, or **mobile push (FCM)**, configure those channels in **Settings**
and opt in from this app.

## Example workflows

**See what you've been told**
- *In the app:* open Notifications for the recent list.
- *Through chat:* "what did you tell me today?", "show my alerts", "any
  notifications I missed?"

**Trace one back**
- *Through chat:* "what reminder was that about?" — each notification links back to
  its source (a reminder, job, etc.).

**Get them on your phone / Discord**
- *In the app/Settings:* enable a channel and opt in, then alerts arrive there too.

## Tips

- Notifications are system-generated — reminders firing and jobs finishing create them automatically.
- Channel choice is per person, so each member gets alerts where they want them.

## Your data

Your notification history is **saved in the database and available to Skipper's
memory**, so "what did you tell me yesterday?" works. Channel credentials (Discord,
Pushover, FCM) live encrypted in Settings; everything stays within your household.
