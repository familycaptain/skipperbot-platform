# Notifications

Your notification center — everything Skipper and its apps want to tell you, in
one place, delivered across your channels.

## What you can do
- **See notifications** from every app (reminders due, jobs done, PM nudges, …).
- **Receive them** on web, Discord, and mobile push depending on what's
  configured.
- **Ask Skipper** — "what notifications do I have?", or "mark all as read".

## Setup
- Channel delivery (Discord, mobile/FCM, Pushover) is configured in Settings;
  in-app notifications work with no setup.
