# Settings

One place to configure Skipper — platform-wide settings, integrations, family
members, and each app's options.

## What you can do
- **System** panel — timezone, default ZIP, AI models, URLs, display/debug flags.
  Settings marked "↻ restart" take effect after a server restart.
- **Integrations** panel — cross-cutting service keys (Discord, Brave, Weather, …).
- **Members** — add/remove family members, set roles, reset passwords (admins);
  change your own password (everyone).
- **Per-app settings** — every app that has options shows its own panel here.
- **Desktop** — show/hide app launcher icons.

## Tips
- Apps with no configurable options don't appear in the list.
